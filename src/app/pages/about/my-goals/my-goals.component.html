My Goals

I worked with small to medium sized to-do lists for a long time, but I decided that having a long list of everything in life at one place is a great way to look back at the list from time to time and access where we stand, how much we got done, what is left to do and plan accordingly. Having said that here is my full list of things I would like to do in my life. Putting this out for the public accomplishes two things, 1. It adds accountability on my part to keep working on this list, to knock things off as well as add new stuff to it, and 2. It helps others out there looking to do something like this, motivates them to do something similar, if not better.
